<h1>Combining Variational Methods with Sampling</h1>

<p>The variational importance sampler is an hybrid algorithm which combines 
sampling with variational approaches to solve intractable integrals, 
particularly in the context of Bayesian inference. </p>

<h3>Software for Microarray Analysis</h3>

<p>We have developed software for image processing of cDNA microarrays using the 
variational importance sampler. The software is automated and provides a higher 
degree of consistency than other approaches.&nbsp; </p>

<p><a href="visVideo.mpg">MPEG video</a> of Software in action on a small 
portion of a slide. Purple lines indicate an initial rough grid layout. Blue 
ovals indicate where the algorithm is looking for the spot. Yellow ovals 
indicate where it finds it.</p>
